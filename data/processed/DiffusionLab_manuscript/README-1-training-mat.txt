1-training
-------------

Trainingset can be loaded in Classification Trainer (for DiffusionLab). Compute the classification tree with the properties:
- nTrackPoints
- MinBoundCircleRadius
- MBCCminusCoM
- entropy
- trackLength
- tortuosity
- wEV1

Categories:
- Immobile
- Hybrid
- mobile

Pixel size: 64 nm
Frame time: 50 ms

100 trajectories were classified in this training set.
